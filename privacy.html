Privacy Policy

Inspirely respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how the app handles information.

Information Collection

Inspirely does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal data.

The app does not require account creation and does not collect:

All user preferences, including selected language, background style, and quote refresh frequency, are stored locally on the user's device. No information is transmitted to external servers.

Third-Party Services

Inspirely does not integrate third-party analytics, advertising networks, or tracking technologies.

Children’s Privacy

Inspirely does not knowingly collect personal information from children or any users.

If future updates introduce features that require data collection, this Privacy Policy will be updated accordingly.
